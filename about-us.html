We operate as Phext, Inc. (bootstrapped C Corp). No VC funding. We ship.

The Substrate: Phext and Coordinated Cognition

Phext is plain text extended by 9 structural delimiters. Each scroll has a coordinate in a 9D lattice—a GPS for knowledge. That makes it addressable, composable, and auditable.

Example: A contract at 1.2.3/4.5.6/7.8.9 can link its negotiation history at 1.2.2/5.1.4/9.1.7. AI-drafted revisions can be tracked to 1.2.4/1.1.1/1.1.2. Every edit, intent, and memory is traceable. Coordinates are internal to files—not global.

  • Continuity: Thoughts have place and history.
  • Legibility: Structure is explicit and navigable.
  • Collaboration: Minds work in parallel without erasure.
  • Alignment: Consent and intent are anchored.

Phext is the shared memory layer of coordinated cognition.
